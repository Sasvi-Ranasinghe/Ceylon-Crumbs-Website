Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Ceylon Crumbs.



🛒 Ordering

How do I place an order online?+
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your delivery details and preferred delivery date. Payment is accepted via card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through our secure PayHere gateway.
What is the minimum order value?+
There is no minimum order value. However, a delivery fee may apply depending on your location and order total. Free delivery is available on orders above a certain amount – check checkout for the current threshold.
Can I modify or cancel my order?+
Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery or collection time. Please call us at +94 11 205 7777 or email us hello@ceyloncrumbs.lk as soon as possible.


🚚 Delivery

Which areas do you deliver to?+
We currently deliver to all areas within the Western Province and select locations island-wide. Delivery availability is shown at checkout based on your address. For areas outside our standard zones, please call us to arrange special delivery.
How far in advance should I order?+
For standard products, we recommend ordering at least 1 day in advance. Custom cakes and large catering orders require a minimum of 3–5 days' notice. During festive seasons (Christmas, Easter, New Year), please order at least 1 week ahead.
Can I pick up my order from a store?+
Yes, collection is available at all our outlets during opening hours. Select "Store Pickup" at checkout and choose your preferred outlet and time.


🎂 Products

Can I customise a cake with a personal message?+
Absolutely! When adding a cake to your cart, you can include personalisation instructions (name, message, colour preferences) in the product notes. For fully custom design cakes, browse our Design Cakes category or contact us directly.
Do your products contain allergens?+
Many of our products contain gluten, dairy, eggs, and nuts. Allergen information is listed on individual product pages. If you have a specific allergy concern, please contact us before ordering so we can advise accordingly.
How long do your products stay fresh?+
All products are baked fresh. Gateaux and cream cakes should be consumed within 2–3 days and stored in the fridge. Savoury items are best eaten on the day of purchase. Pound cakes and loaves stay fresh for up to 5 days in an airtight container.
What are the Luxe Gateau options?+
Our Luxe Gateau collection features premium handcrafted gateaux including the Espresso Dream Tiramisu, Velvet Charm Red Velvet, Infinite Rainbow, and Harmony Layers Chocolate Opera. Visit our All Products page to explore the full collection.


💳 Payment

What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept Visa, MasterCard, and American Express through our secure online payment gateway (PayHere). Cash on delivery is also available for select areas. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Is online payment secure?+
Yes. We use PayHere, one of Sri Lanka's leading payment gateways, which is PCI DSS compliant. Your card details are never stored on our servers.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you quickly.
