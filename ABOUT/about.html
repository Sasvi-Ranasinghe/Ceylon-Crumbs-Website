Our Story

Crafted with Passion and Purpose

Ceylon Crumbs was created with a simple idea to bring people together through food that feels special, comforting, and memorable. What started as a small passion project has grown into a brand that celebrates both creativity and quality. From the very beginning, our goal has been to offer more than just baked goods. We aim to create experiences that stay with our customers long after the last bite.

Today, Ceylon Crumbs is known for a wide and exciting range of offerings, including flavourful savouries, beautifully designed cakes, rich gateaux, delightful sweets, satisfying lunch options, refreshing beverages, as well as our popular cheesecakes and bento cakes. Each product is thoughtfully prepared to suit both everyday cravings and special occasions.

We use carefully selected ingredients, combining local freshness with premium imports to ensure every item meets our standards. Behind every creation is a passionate team dedicated to consistency, taste, and presentation making sure each product reflects the quality we stand for.

Whether it is a small personal treat, a celebration cake, or a complete meal, we approach every order with care and attention. At Ceylon Crumbs, every customer matters, and every order is an opportunity to deliver something truly special.
